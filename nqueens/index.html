N-Queens Solver

In the game of chess, the queen can move in a straight line in any direction until she reaches the edge of the board or another piece. The classical puzzle called 8-queens is one that asks the solver to place 8 queens onto a standard 8x8 chessboard such that no two queens are able to capture eachother (cannot move into eachother). I have chosen to extend this problem into n-queens - place n queens onto an nxn chessboard with no possible collisions by the queens.
