N-Queens Solver

In the game of chess, the queen can move in a straight line in any direction until she reaches the edge of the board or another piece. The classical puzzle called 8-queens is one that asks the solver to place 8 queens onto a standard 8x8 chessboard such that no two queens are able to capture eachother (cannot move into eachother). This is an extention to 8-queens, "N-queens"! Place N queens onto an NxN chessboard such that no two queens can capture each other.

Enter a number you wish to use as n and a solution to the problem will appear below.
