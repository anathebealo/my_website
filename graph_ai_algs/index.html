Coordinates Representation

Represent edges as: (<from_node_name>, <to_node_name>, <weight>)
OR
(<from_node_name>, <to_node_name>)

Total entry has the format:
<number_of_nodes> [<names_of_nodes_comma_separates>] (<edge_data>) () ...

graph represented above is: 3 [a,b,c] (a,a,4) (b,a,3) (b,b,3)
and without edge weights: 3 [a,b,c] (a,a) (b,a) (b,b)
