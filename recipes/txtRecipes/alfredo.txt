title
Alfredo Sauce
date
7/28/16
source
Food.com
photo, 1
images/alfredo.jpg
ingredients
7
3,tbsp unsalted butter
2,tbsp olive oil
2,garlic cloves minced
2,cups heavy cream
1/4,tsp white pepper
1/2,cup grated parmesan cheese
3/4,cup mozzarella cheese
directions
Melt butter in a medium saucepan with the olive oil over medium-low heat.
Add the garlic, white pepper and cream. Bring the mixture to a simmer. Stir often to avoid burnt/gross cream.
Add the parmesan cheese and simmer sauce for 8-10 minutes to thicken sauce. Make sure it is smooth.
When sauce has thickened, add the mozzarella cheese and stir <strong>frequently</strong> until smooth.
Serve over noodles
