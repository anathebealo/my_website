Love Lemon Bars

ingredients
10
1,cup butter
2,cups flour
1/2,cups confectioners' sugar
4,eggs
2,cups sugar
1/4,cup flour
1,tsp baking powder
6,tblsp fresh lemon juice
grated peel of <strong>1</strong> lemon
confectioners' sugar
directions
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Blend 2 cups flour, butter and confectioners' sugar thoroughly.
Press dough into a well greased and floured 13x9x2-inch pan.
Bake for 25 minutes in preheated oven (watch closely! Make sure it doesn't burn!)
Beat eggs until frothy. Add all remaining ingredients and beat until smooth.
Pour mixture into the crust and bake 20-25 minutes at 350 degrees.
Dust with confectioners' sugar and cut into squares.
Refridgerate.
