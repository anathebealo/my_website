title
Blueberry Muffins
date
7/28/16
source
Mom
photo, 1
images/blueberryMuffins.jpg
ingredients
11
1/2,cup butter or vegetable oil
1,cup sugar
2,eggs
2,cups flour
2,tsp baking powder
1/2,tsp salt
1/2,tsp cinnamon
1,tsp vanilla extract
1/2,cup milk
2,cups blueberries (fresh or frozen)
2,tsp sugar
directions
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Cream butter and sugar. Add the eggs one at a time and mix until blended.
Fold in the dry ingredients, vanilla, blueberries and milk.
Grease 12 muffin tins (or use cupcake liners). 
Pile muffin batter high in tins. 
Sprinklesugar on tops.
Bake in oven for 25-30 minutes.
