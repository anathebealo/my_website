title
Hot Fudge Sauce
date
7/28/16
source
Better Homes and Gardens
photo, 1
images/chocolateSauce.jpg
ingredients
4
3/4,cup semisweet chocolate chips
1/2,stick (1/4 cup) unsalted butter
2/3,cup sugar
1,5-ounce can evaporated milk
directions
In a small saucepan melt the chocolate and the butter over medium heat until melted together and stirs smooth.
Add the sugar.
Gradually stir in the evaporated milk.
Bring the mixture to a boil and then reduce heat.
Boil <strong>gently</strong> over low heat for 8 minutes (make sure to stir frequently so it doesn't burn).
Remove from heat and cool slightly.
Serve warm over ice cream!
