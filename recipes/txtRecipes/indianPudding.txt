title
Indian Pudding
date
7/15/16
source
Family Recipe -- from Barbara McCann
photo, 1
images/indianPudding.jpg
ingredients
10
4,level tbsp cornmeal
1,egg
3,cups whole milk
1,cup whole milk
1/2,cup molasses (scant)
1/2,cup sugar (scant)
1/2,tsp ginger
1/2,tsp cinnamon
1/4,tsp salt
dash,nutmeg
directions
Preheat oven to 325 degrees
Combine 3 cups milk, cornmeal, sugar, ginger, cinnamon, salt and nutmeg.
Put mixture into pot on stove and cook until it reaches a rolling boil.
Pour mixture into a buttered baking dish and put into preheated oven.
Mix egg and remaining milk together.
When mixture in oven starts to boil, stir in milk/egg mix.
Continue to cook for 1-2 hours or until the top looks cracked and dark brown.
Serve warm with vanilla ice cream.
