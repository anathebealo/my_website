title
Vegetable Dip
date
8/23/16
source
Original
photo, 1
images/veggieDip.jpg
ingredients
7
1,cup sour cream
1,cup light mayonnaise
2/3,cup fresh dill
1/2,shallot
1,tsp onion powder
1/2,tsp salt
1/2,tsp freshly ground pepper
directions
Finely chop dill and shallots.
Mix all ingredients together well.
Chill and serve
