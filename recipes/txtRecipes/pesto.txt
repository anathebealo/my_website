ingredients
7
1,cup packed fresh basil leaves
3,oz pine nuts
5,oz pine nuts
2,garlic cloves
1/2,cup olive oil
Salt
Pepper
directions
Put basil, 3oz pine nuts, garlic and half of the olive oil into a food processor.
Blend until mixed well
Stir reminaing pine nuts and other 1/4 cup olive oil into mixture.
Add salt and pepper to taste
Cover and store, or serve immediately.
